Yes: 15, 23, 39, 62, 86, 98
No: 4, 10, 19, 27, 32, 44, 49, 53, 57, 66, 71, 77, 82, 89, 93


Please read each statement carefully and select Yes or No based on how well it describes you. There are no right or wrong answers.
